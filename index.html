Monthly Bootcamp - $115

Our monthly bootcamp is designed to give you a comprehensive understanding of Web Development. This is geared towards new and experienced developers who want to either get a general understanding of main web technologies, or focus on an area of weakness to improve. We will meet for 4 hours a week and you will given challenges and sites to build throughout the bootcamp. We are always available to hop into a voice call and help you with any problem you run into.

2 Week Intesive Bootcamp - $75/hr

This intensive program is built for those that are looking for a challenge. If you want to improve at a faster pace, get experience with hands on projects and have the time to do so, this course is for you. We will focus on rapid prototyping and building out projects with Vanilla JS, HTML and CSS. We can give a short dive into any framework or library you are interested. Our goal is to expose you to the all the different fields in web development and prepare you for the job market. This plan is built to develop your skills at a rapid pace so you can build more advanced projects on your own.

5 Day Crash Course

Our original course has been carried over to offer those that are new to web development or only have done a few projects or know nothing at all. We will walk you through the basics of HTML5, CSS3 and JS. We want to provide you with the crucial foundation you need so you can keep expanding your knowledge as you grow as a developer. We will focus on rapid prototyping and building landing pages, portfolio projects and sites for made up clients to give you real world experience.
